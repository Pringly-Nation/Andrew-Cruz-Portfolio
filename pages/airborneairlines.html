Project Overview

Airborne Airlines is a website to provide luxury flights and services to people.

Contribution

I worked with Airborne Airlines to create an interactive prototype for them to based their website off of. This included design research, thinking and creating the prototype.

Research

Research involved researching competitors such as Qantas and Jetstar and seeing what they have included in their website layout. Seeing their alignment decisions, animation decisions and general vibe they are going for informed me of what design decisions I need to incorporate to provide Airborne Airlines a luxurious feeling website.
