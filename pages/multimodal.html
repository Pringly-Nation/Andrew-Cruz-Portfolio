Project Overview

The Multimodal Candy Carousel is a conceptualised design for users to pick up candy through a holographic display that uses the principles of Gestural Design by John Underkoffler

Contribution

While my group members were more artistically gifted than me and contributed there, I contributed through the thinking of what actual functionality is going to be inside the multimodal experience as well as creating the documentation for the presentation.

Research

After feedback from tutors and peers, many iterations of the multimodal experience came to fruition.

Main Design Elements

Hands and Space, Body and Display

Use of gestural commands to interact with the experience.

Agency

Aesthetics

Data Mobility

Ability to move data from different screens without any time delays and additional technologies required.

Plurality

The ability to have multiple users using the multimodal experience at a time. Allowing interactivitiy between users.
